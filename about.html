Our Roots

Tucked away in the serene embrace of Gujarat’s countryside, Goradka is more than just a village—it is a living tapestry of traditions, smiles, and shared memories. Golden fields sway gently in the breeze, while the banyan-shaded chowk becomes the heart of evening conversations. The aroma of freshly brewed tea mingles with the distant sound of temple bells, and every path seems to lead to a story told by its people. Here, life moves at its own gentle pace, bound together by trust, warmth, and the laughter of children playing under the wide open skies. To visit Goradka is to feel the soul of rural Gujarat—simple, vibrant, and timeless.

People: Total population: 5,449 Male: 2,777
Female: 2,672

The people of Goradka are the heart of the village. Every smile tells a story of warmth and kindness. They work hard in the fields under the golden sun, yet always find time to help their neighbors. In joy, they celebrate together; in hardship, they stand united. Here, every guest is welcomed as one of their own.

Houses:Households 989

The houses of Goradka reflect the village’s charm. Some are built with modern designs, others with traditional walls of mud and brick. Colorful doors and windows brighten every street. Courtyards are filled with plants, grain baskets, and children’s laughter. Many homes have verandas where families gather in the evenings. Each house tells a story of love, tradition, and togetherness.

Area:Total geographic area: 1,049.14 hectares (~10.49 km²)

Goradka spreads peacefully across Gujarat’s green countryside. Golden fields surround the village on every side. Narrow lanes lead to the bustling central chowk. Shaded trees offer rest to travelers and villagers alike. The air is fresh, carrying the scent of crops and earth. Every corner of the area feels calm, welcoming, and full of life.
