Our Roots

Goradka is a small and peaceful village in Gujarat with a close-knit community. The village is known for its cultural traditions, strong unity among its people, and scenic beauty. Surrounded by fields and trees, it offers a calm and simple lifestyle rooted in values.

People: Over 1,500 residents live here with strong community bonds and cultural pride.

The people of Goradka actively participate in festivals, farming, and support village development activities. Their hospitality and unity are remarkable.

Houses: Most homes are traditional and eco-friendly, built using local materials.

Homes are typically built from mud bricks and lime plaster, helping regulate temperature naturally. Many families also maintain small gardens.

Area: The village spans ~3 sq. km surrounded by green farmland.

The region includes schools, temples, and a few small shops. It's a quiet, nature-rich area that supports agriculture and livestock.
