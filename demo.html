About Goradka

Goradka is a peaceful village known for its scenic beauty, cultural heritage, and rich history. Explore the interactive map below to discover key locations in our village.

🕉️ Village Temple

The ancient Shiva temple is the spiritual heart of Goradka, built over 200 years ago. Locals gather here for daily prayers and festivals.

📍 Central Goradka | Built: 1823 | Festivals: Mahashivratri, Shravan

🏞️ Goradka Lake

A serene lake surrounded by lush greenery, perfect for evening walks and bird watching. Home to various migratory birds during winter.

📍 East Goradka | Area: 5 acres | Best time: Evening

🏫 Primary School

Goradka Primary School educates children from nearby villages. Recently renovated with modern facilities and digital classrooms.

📍 South Goradka | Students: 150+ | Grades: 1-7

🛒 Weekly Market

Every Tuesday, vendors from surrounding areas gather to sell fresh produce, handicrafts, and daily essentials.

📍 Market Square | Day: Tuesday | Time: 8 AM - 6 PM

🏥 Health Center

24/7 health services with qualified doctors and nurses. Provides emergency care and regular health checkups for villagers.

📍 West Goradka | Staff: 5 | Services: Emergency, General Care

🌳 Community Park

A beautifully maintained park with children's playground, walking tracks, and outdoor exercise equipment for all ages.

📍 North Goradka | Area: 2 acres | Features: Playground, Gym
